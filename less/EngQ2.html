LESSON 2 (VERBS)

III. Quiz

Post Activity

Find the verbs, then place them before the number.
1. They all ran to school together.
2. The bus drives way too fast sometimes.
3. Their new dog barks all the time.
4. Peter plays soccer every Sunday.
5. Her new car is fast.
6. The family play board games every weekend.
7. He buys clothes once a month.
8. Sarah ate too much cake last night.
9. Mitchel works 60 hours a week.
10. Mary is kind to everyone she meets.
