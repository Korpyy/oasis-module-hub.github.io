Lesson 2: BINOMIAL

III. Quiz

Direction: Answer the following question.


1. How many terms will be there in a binomial expression?
a. 4
b. 3
c. 2
d. 1

2. Identify the binomial from the following.
a. 3x
b. 4xy
c. 2x - 5xy
d. x2 - 4x + 5y

3. Addition of two binomial expressions may result in
a. zero
b. one term
c. two terms
d. either of the above

4. Subtraction of two binomial expressions may result in
a. two terms
b. one term
c. zero
d. either of the above

5. What will be the maximum number of terms in the multiplication of two binomials?
a. 4
b. 5
c. 3
d. 2
